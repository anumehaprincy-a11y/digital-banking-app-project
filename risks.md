# Risks & Mitigation

## Risk 1: Delay in feature development  
Mitigation: Prioritize high-impact tasks and monitor progress closely  

## Risk 2: Integration challenges between features  
Mitigation: Conduct early testing and align dependencies  

## Risk 3: Unclear requirements  
Mitigation: Define user stories clearly and validate assumptions  

## Risk 4: Resource constraints  
Mitigation: Optimize workload and adjust sprint priorities
